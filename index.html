History of our Institute

Cumilla Shikkha Board Govt. Model College is situated in the most charming environment of North Shaktala adjacent to Katobari Road in the southern part of Comilla was nationalized on 18 September 2017. It commenced from the academic year 2008-2009 through admission in class xi in Humanities, Business studies and Science. Based on nearly three acres of land with eye-catching intra-structure and serene atmosphere, the institution facilitates education from class VI to XII with boarding facilities
